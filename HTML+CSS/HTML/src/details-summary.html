What is Netflix ?
Netflix is a subscription-based streaming service that allows users to watch TV shows, movies, anime, documentaries, and more on internet-connected devices.
How much does Netflix cost ?
Netflix subscription prices in India range from ₹149 to ₹649 per month depending on the plan. The Mobile plan is ₹149, Basic is ₹199, Standard is ₹499, and Premium is ₹649.
